ABOUT US

WHO WE ARE

HanapGimik is your ultimate go-to hub for discovering the best events, hangouts, and happenings around the city. Whether you're looking for weekend gigs, food festivals, local markets, or underground parties—we’ve got you covered.

Born from the idea that fun shouldn’t be hard to find, HanapGimik connects event-goers with event creators through a vibrant, user-friendly platform. No more endless scrolling—just scroll, tap, and go!

OUR MISSION

To make event discovery and booking easy, exciting, and accessible for everyone who’s always on the lookout for their next gimik.

WHAT MAKES US DIFFERENT?

• Curated and crowd-sourced events for every vibe
• A platform that supports local organizers and creative communities
• Hassle-free booking and real-time updates
• Visually-driven interface—because we love aesthetic posters too!

OUR STORY

HanapGimik started as a small idea between friends who loved exploring new places, local gigs, food spots, and weekend hangouts.

We realized many people struggle to find events happening nearby—so we built a platform that connects people to experiences they’ll love.

From a simple passion project, HanapGimik has grown into a community-driven hub supporting local creators, artists, organizers, and everyone looking for their next unforgettable gimik.
